Simulate Physiological conditions

The Current Version of VascularSim supports simulation of stenosis condition in all the arteries in the human body.


Stenosis is modelled as a percentage reduction in the radius of the artery.


More physiological conditions such as the effect of Baroreflex and Respiratory system on the artery tree are to be added soon.
